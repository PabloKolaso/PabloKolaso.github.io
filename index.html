About Me

I've been working as a Tier 2 Support Technician at Ness Technologies, on-site at Applied Materials in Rehovot. Day to day that means handling escalated issues, managing Active Directory — accounts, permissions, GPOs — and doing field work at company branches: setting up workstations, configuring hardware, sorting out connectivity. I also studied SCCM during my engineering technician program, covering software deployment and endpoint management.

Before that, two years at Naamat Technological College earning my Engineering Technician certificate in Network Engineering and Information Security. CCNA in depth, intro to CCNP, Python and SQL on the side. My high school final project was designing a full multi-branch corporate network across three countries — servers, routers, switches, VLANs, OSPF, NAT, the works. That project is what made me realize this is genuinely what I want to do.

I figure things out, don't leave problems half-solved, and get along well with people — technical and non-technical alike. Russian and Hebrew natively, strong English. Always looking for the next challenge.
